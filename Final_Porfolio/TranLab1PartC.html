Delicious Flan Recipes

Ingredients :

  • 3 Eggs
  • 12 ounces of milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanila Extract

Directions :

  1. Preheat the oven up to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Turn on the heat to medium-low level, put sugar into the pan and add a bit of water until the sugar turn golden (like caramel)

  3. Pour that syrup into another glass container and set it aside
  4. In another bowl, beat the eggs. Put milk and vanila extract into the bowl. Mixed well.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the glass container and cover it with aluminum foil
  6. Bake in the oven up to 60 minutes
  7. Cool down and serve with condense milk or any fruits of your chocies
